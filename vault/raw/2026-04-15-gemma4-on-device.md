## What is Gemma4?

Google's open-source AI model (Apache 2.0), with mobile-optimized variant "Gemma4-E2B-it" (~2.5GB). Runs locally on smartphones without cloud connectivity.

## Setup

Install "Google AI Edge Gallery" app from Google Play or App Store, download model over Wi-Fi.

## Capabilities

- Text chat ("AI Chat") function
- Image analysis ("Ask Image") function
- No internet connection required during use

## Performance

"Lightweight model delivers responses that don't feel significantly inferior to cloud-based AI" — operates with surprising speed.

## Limitations

- Knowledge cutoff around early 2025
- Occasional unnatural Japanese phrasing
- No chat history retention

## Significance

Fundamental shift in AI accessibility: immediate, offline responses on consumer devices. Represents future where AI is truly on-device.
