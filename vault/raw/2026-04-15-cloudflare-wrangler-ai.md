## Overview

Cloudflare announced plans to rebuild its Wrangler CLI to serve as a unified interface across the entire Cloudflare platform, specifically optimized for AI agent usage.

## Key Points

**Previous Limitations:**
The existing Wrangler tool only supported a subset of Cloudflare's offerings. As the company noted: "Many Cloudflare products have no CLI commands in Wrangler. And agents love CLIs."

**New Approach:**
- Rebuilt Wrangler CLI covering commands for every Cloudflare service
- Infrastructure-as-code configuration management across the platform
- Early technical preview now available

**Why AI Agents:**
- Agents prefer CLIs over GUIs
- Enables direct filesystem inspection, agent operation debugging
- Eliminates friction in agentic workflows

Initiative aligns with Cloudflare's "AgentsWeek" focus.
