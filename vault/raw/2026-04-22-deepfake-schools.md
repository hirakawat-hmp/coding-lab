# Deepfake Abuse in Schools Global Crisis — Raw

source: https://wired.jp/article/deepfake-nudify-schools-global-crisis/
fetched: 2026-04-22

## Summary

WIRED/Indicator joint investigation reveals AI-generated sexual deepfakes affecting ~600 students across ~90 schools in 28+ countries since 2023.

## Key Facts

- Scale: at least 28 countries documented; ~600 students across ~90 schools (likely underreported)
- Primary victims: young women and girls; teachers increasingly targeted
- Technology: "nudification" apps with minimal technical skill required; dozens of services online
- Perpetrators: predominantly teenage boys (humiliation, revenge, peer pressure)
- Impact: psychological distress, school avoidance, fear of permanent internet circulation
- Institutional response: inconsistent; delayed investigations documented
- Emerging solutions: UK/Europe considering bans; Australia's eSafety Commissioner taking enforcement action
