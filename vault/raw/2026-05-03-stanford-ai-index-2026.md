# Stanford AI Index 2026 (2026-05-03)

Source: https://spectrum.ieee.org/state-of-ai-index-2026
Also: https://www.unite.ai/stanford-ai-index-2026-reveals-a-field-racing-ahead-of-its-guardrails/

Published April 13, 2026 by Stanford's Institute for Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence.

Key findings:
- World AI compute capacity increased 3x/year since 2022; total AI compute increased 30x since 2021
- US-China AI capability gap reduced to 2.7 percentage points (benchmark advantage)
- Foundation Model Transparency Index dropped from 58 to 40; Google, Anthropic, OpenAI no longer disclose dataset sizes or training duration for latest models
- High-quality text data may run out; this drives synthetic data and more efficient training methods
- Junior developer employment down 20%
- AI capabilities racing ahead of governance frameworks
