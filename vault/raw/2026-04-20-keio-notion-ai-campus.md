## Overview
Keio University (慶應義塾) implementing Notion across all faculty and staff to consolidate 168 years of accumulated intellectual assets and feed them to AI systems.

## Key Details
- Partnership: Notion Labs Japan + Keio University strategic MOU
- Scale: All teaching and administrative staff (university-wide)
- Timeline: 3-year goal for "world-class AI environment"
- Goal: Unified management of dispersed documents, regulations, knowledge, records, operational information

## Four Pillars
1. Integrated knowledge and operations management platform
2. Organizational productivity and AI transformation
3. Student AI literacy promotion
4. Domestic and international sharing of outcomes

## Core Objective
Eliminate "work about work" by enabling shared context between humans and AI, allowing staff to focus on education and research rather than information retrieval.
