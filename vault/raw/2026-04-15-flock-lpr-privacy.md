## What is Flock Safety?

Flock Safety provides License Plate Reader (LPR) technology. Systems capture images of vehicle characteristics in public spaces. Primarily serves law enforcement and security organizations.

## The Privacy Opt-Out Attempt

A California resident submitted a CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) privacy deletion request to Flock, asking to remove all personal information about himself, his vehicle, and household members.

## Flock's Response

Declined the request, arguing they operate as a "service provider and processor" for their customers (law enforcement agencies), who technically own the data. Recommended contacting the contracting organization instead.

## Legal Concern

The author found this legally questionable under CCPA requirements, indicating he may pursue legal counsel.

## Data Retention

Default: 30-day rolling retention for license plate images.
