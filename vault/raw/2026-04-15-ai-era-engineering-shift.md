## Main Argument

As AI makes application development faster, engineers must shift focus from "how to build" to "how to deliver." True value lies in release strategies and architectural design, not just implementation.

## Core Realization

"Start (problem) and goal (release format) understood means you can talk to customers"

Knowing multiple deployment options enables better client conversations and solutions.

## Release Strategy Diversity

- Web/Notion for internal documentation
- Chrome extensions/Slack bots for productivity
- APIs/Google Apps Script for external integrations

## Evolving Engineer Roles

| Phase | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| Prompt Engineer | Quality instructions |
| Context Engineering | Communication flow design |
| Harness Engineering | Orchestrating AI + external systems |

## Concrete Example

Service automating email intake and AI-powered talent matching: zero to production in ~4 days (traditionally months).

## Infrastructure Evolution

IaC/Docker, BaaS (Firebase/Supabase/Amplify), Vercel, Google Apps Script
