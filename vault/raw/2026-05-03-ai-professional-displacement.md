# AIは専門職まで奪うのか？ - WIRED Japan (2026-05-03)

Source: https://wired.jp/article/sz-can-ai-be-pro-worker/
Published: 2026-05-02 (WIRED Japan)
OGP: https://media.wired.jp/photos/69f0cea103c1ed9be85cf714/master/w_2560%2Cc_limit/COPYCassidy_AI_GettyImages-2204928658.jpg

MIT economists Daron Acemoglu, Simon Johnson, David Autor featured.

Key points:
- Wall Street volatility from AI employment impact fears
- Economists argue: "technology change cannot be stopped, but its shape can be determined"
- Government intervention needed via procurement power and tax reform
- Example: Schneider Electric "Electrician's Assistant" - AI augments expertise rather than replacing
- Concern: companies harvesting worker knowledge to build automation systems (like historical enclosure movements)

Policy proposals:
- Wage insurance for displaced workers
- Universal basic capital funds
- Tax restructuring to incentivize labor-friendly AI over pure automation

Stanford AI Index 2026: Junior developer employment down 20%.
