# EU Replaceable Battery Regulation 2027 — Raw Source

Source: https://www.theolivepress.es/spain-news/2026/04/20/eu-to-force-replaceable-batteries-in-phones-and-tablets-from-2027/
Fetched: 2026-04-21
Published: April 20, 2026

## Key Facts

- Effective: February 18, 2027
- Scope: All smartphones and tablets sold in EU
- Requirements:
  - User-removable batteries without specialized tools
  - If tools required: provided free with purchase
  - Replacement batteries available for 5+ years after last unit sold
  - System updates required for 5+ years
  - USB-C charging mandated (devices manufactured after 2024)
- Market: ~150M smartphones + ~24M tablets sold annually in EU
- Environmental goal: €20B savings for consumers by 2030; reduce e-waste (currently ~40% recycling rate)
