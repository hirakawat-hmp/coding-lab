# Oscars Ban AI Actors and Scripts (2026-05-03)

Source: https://www.afpbb.com/articles/-/3633832
Published: 2026-05-02

Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences announced new eligibility rules:
- Only human actors with legal credits and personal consent qualify for acting awards
- Screenwriting must be authored by humans, not AI systems
- "Firm measures" against unrestricted AI use in entertainment

Context: Following AI version of late actor Val Kilmer appearing in trailer for "As Deep as the Grave".

Background: AI in entertainment was central concern in 2023 actors/screenwriters strikes. Deezer reported 44% of daily uploads are AI-generated music.

Also: Spotify "Verified by Spotify" badge (from yesterday's digest) is a parallel movement.
