## Overview

Marvelous Designer 2026.0 is the first major update of 2026 for the clothing-specialized 3D modeling and simulation software.

## Key New Features

- **Lacing Tool**: Create intricate lacing structures along eyelets (shoes, corsets, etc.)
- **3D Pencil**: Draw lines directly on 3D avatars to automatically generate 2D patterns
- **3D Shape Preservation During 2D Pattern Deformation**: Maintain 3D geometry while resizing 2D patterns
- **EveryWear Template Rigging (Beta)**: Automate rig generation and skinning customized to clothing types
- **Toon Shader**: Apply toon rendering effects for stylized visualization
