# Raw: Gemini Personal Intelligence in Japan

Source: https://www.itmedia.co.jp/aiplus/articles/2604/15/news070.html
Fetched: 2026-04-15
Published: April 15, 2026

## Overview

Google launched Personal Intelligence (パーソナルインテリジェンス) for Gemini in Japan, starting April 14, 2026. Available across Web, Android, iOS for AI Plus, Pro, and Ultra subscribers.

## Functionality

Enables Gemini to access user data from Google services and "retrieve information from your personal Google apps, then reason through it to deliver more thoughtful, custom responses."

## Supported Integrations

- Google Workspace (Gmail, Calendar, Keep, Tasks, Docs, Drive)
- Google Photos
- YouTube Music
- GitHub
- SynthID

## Configuration

Manage via settings; toggle service access individually. Available in Gemini Pro and via Settings & Help section.

## Privacy

- User data managed under strict Google security standards
- Data not exposed to third parties
- Special protections for sensitive data categories
- User consent required for processing
