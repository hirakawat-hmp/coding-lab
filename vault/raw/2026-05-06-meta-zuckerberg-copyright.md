# Zuckerberg 'Personally Authorized' Meta's Copyright Infringement, Publishers Say

Source: https://apnews.com/article/meta-mark-zuckerberg-ai-publishers-lawsuit-llama-5609846d4d840014974a847b01079c32
Retrieved: 2026-05-06

## Summary

Publishers and authors allege in a lawsuit that Meta CEO Mark Zuckerberg personally authorized and encouraged the use of copyrighted materials for AI training without license or compensation, specifically for the Llama model series.

## Key Points

- Zuckerberg allegedly personally directed the use of pirated books for Llama training
- Publishers filing suit include multiple major media entities
- Allegations contradict Meta's public stance that training data is publicly available
- Scott Turow (Authors Guild president) among plaintiffs
- Published: May 5, 2026

## Context

The case is part of a broader wave of copyright litigation against AI companies using books, articles, and other creative works for model training without permission or compensation.
