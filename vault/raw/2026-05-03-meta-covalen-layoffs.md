# Meta/Covalen AI Worker Layoffs (2026-05-03)

Source: https://wired.jp/article/meta-covalen-ai-workers-layoffs/
Published: 2026-05-02
OGP: https://media.wired.jp/photos/69f3322728bbf1da37b380eb/master/w_2560%2Cc_limit/Hundreds-of-Workers-Training-Meta-AI-Are-Being-Laid-Off-Business-1246766082.jpg

Meta's outsourcing partner Covalen (Dublin) laying off 700+ workers engaged in AI model improvement.
- ~500 are data annotators reviewing AI-generated content for safety compliance
- Second round of cuts within 6 months (November 2025: ~400 workers)
- Combined with November cuts, Dublin staff will be nearly halved
- Workers informed via brief video call with no opportunity for questions

Key issue: 6-month "cooldown period" restricting reemployment at Meta competitors.

Worker quote: "We're essentially training AI to replace ourselves by providing correct answers."

International labor unions: demand right to refuse training replacement AI systems.

Meta statement: reducing outsourcing dependence while strengthening internal systems and deploying advanced AI for content management.
