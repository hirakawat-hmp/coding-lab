## Key Facts

- Major media blocking Internet Archive crawlers: NYT, The Guardian, USA Today, Reddit
- 100+ journalists signed open letter protesting
- AI companies exploit archived content when original sites block them → publishers extended defenses to Archive

## Why Archives Matter

- Policy journalists: historical government documents
- Investigative: deleted/removed materials
- Public records deletion backup
- Cultural history

## Additional Challenges

1. Dynamic web pages (JavaScript-heavy) not fully capturable
2. Walled social platforms (Discord, Instagram, Facebook)
3. Paywall expansion

## Japan Context

- Major newspapers (Asahi, Yomiuri) have minimal archiving
- No US-style fair use → legal gray areas for preservation
