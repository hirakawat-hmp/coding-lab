# Anthropic: Higher Usage Limits for Claude and a Compute Deal with SpaceX

Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/higher-limits-spacex
Retrieved: 2026-05-07

## Key Announcements (May 6, 2026)

Anthropic announced increased capacity through a SpaceX partnership and corresponding service upgrades. The company secured access to "all of the compute capacity at their Colossus 1 data center," providing over 220,000 NVIDIA GPUs and 300+ megawatts of new computing power.

## Service Improvements

Three changes take effect immediately:

1. Claude Code rate limits doubled for Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise plans across five-hour windows
2. Peak hours restrictions eliminated for Pro and Max Claude Code users
3. API rate limits substantially increased for Claude Opus models

## Infrastructure Expansion

Beyond the SpaceX deal, Anthropic is advancing multiple compute agreements:
- Amazon partnership (up to 5 gigawatts, with ~1 GW by end of 2026)
- Google and Broadcom partnership (5 GW launching 2027)
- Microsoft and NVIDIA strategic partnership ($30 billion Azure capacity)
- $50 billion Fluidstack infrastructure investment

## Global Strategy

The company emphasizes international expansion for regulated sectors (finance, healthcare, government) requiring regional data residency compliance. Anthropic also committed to covering electricity price increases from U.S. data centers.
