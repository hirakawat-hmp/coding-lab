# Anthropic Surpasses OpenAI with $1 Trillion Secondary Market Valuation

Source: https://finance.yahoo.com/markets/stocks/articles/anthropic-beats-openai-secondary-markets-213828157.html
Retrieved: 2026-05-06

## Summary

Anthropic has reached an implied valuation of $1 trillion on secondary markets, overtaking OpenAI which trades at approximately $880 billion on Forge Global. In Japanese media reported as 141兆円 (approximately $1T at current exchange rates).

## Key Facts

- Secondary market valuation: ~$1 trillion (implied)
- OpenAI secondary market: ~$880 billion
- Anthropic's latest primary round (February 2026): $380 billion post-money
- Revenue growth: $9B annualized (end-2025) → $30B (March 2026), 233% growth in one quarter
- Primary driver: strong demand for Claude Code and coding tools

## Important Context

- Secondary market trades are illiquid minority positions with no board rights
- Primary market valuation anchored at $380B (Feb 2026 round)
- IPO exploration: possible late 2026, advisors Goldman Sachs and JPMorgan
- Target IPO valuation: $400-500B range
