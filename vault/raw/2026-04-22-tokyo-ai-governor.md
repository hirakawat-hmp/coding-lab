# Tokyo AI Governor Avatar — Raw

source: https://www.itmedia.co.jp/news/articles/2604/21/news134.html
fetched: 2026-04-22

## Summary

Tokyo Metropolitan Government launched "AI Governor Information Corner" — an AI avatar voice service using Governor Yuriko Koike's voice/likeness. Published April 21, 2026.

## Key Facts

- Service: "Yuriko's Voice" (ゆりこの声) console on Tokyo's official website
- Languages: English, French, Spanish, Arabic, multiple Japanese dialects
- Platform: Metropolitan website + social media accounts
- Coverage: diverse city services (welfare to cultural initiatives)
- Management interface included for AI information corner
