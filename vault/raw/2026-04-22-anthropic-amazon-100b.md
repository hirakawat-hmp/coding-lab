## Summary

Anthropic and Amazon announced expansion of strategic partnership with significant capital commitments on April 21, 2026.

- Anthropic: investing $100B+ over 10 years to procure Amazon AI chips (up to 5 gigawatts)
- Amazon: $5B initial investment + up to $20B additional based on commercial milestones = $25B total
- Project Rainier: already operates 1M+ Trainium2 chips
- Trainium3: scheduled for large-scale deployment H2 2026
- Claude: now accessible on all three major clouds (AWS via Bedrock, Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure)
- Anthropic also pursuing Google TPU partnership (announced April 6, 2026)
