## Key Facts

- NVIDIA market cap: $4T+ due to AI chip dominance
- Advantage maintained through software ecosystem (code optimization)

### Emerging Competitors

- **Wafer** (Code Optimization): AI models for hardware-specific software optimization; raised $4M seed from Google's Jeff Dean and OpenAI's Wojciech Zaremba; CEO Emilio Andler: "maximize intelligence per watt"
- **Ricursive Intelligence** (Chip Design Automation): ex-Google engineers; raised $335M in months; $4B valuation; automates chip design with LLMs

### AI Democratization Trends

- AMD, Amazon Trainium, Google TPU now match NVIDIA's theoretical compute performance
- AI models (including Claude) demonstrate superhuman coding → potentially eliminates NVIDIA's software advantage
- Automated chip design enables more companies to develop custom processors
