# Deezer: 44% of Daily Uploads Are AI-Generated — Raw Source

Source: https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/20/deezer-says-44-of-songs-uploaded-to-its-platform-daily-are-ai-generated/
Fetched: 2026-04-21

## Key Facts

- ~75,000 AI-generated tracks uploaded daily (2M+/month)
- 44% of new uploads are AI-generated (up from 10,000/day in Jan 2025)
- AI music = only 1–3% of total streams
- 85% of AI-generated streams detected as fraudulent and demonetized
- Deezer started tagging AI tracks June 2025 (first major streaming service)
- 13.4M+ AI tracks tagged throughout 2025
- 97% of survey participants couldn't distinguish AI from human music
- AI tracks excluded from algorithmic recommendations and editorial playlists
- AI-generated track recently topped iTunes charts in multiple countries
