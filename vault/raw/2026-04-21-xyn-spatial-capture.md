## Three Components

1. **XYN Spatial Scan Navi** — smartphone app for α-series camera spatial capture with AR navigation, real-time preview, smart assist
2. **XYN Spatial Scan** — cloud app using Sony proprietary algorithms for precise shape/texture estimation
3. **XYN Spatial Renderer Plugin (beta)** — rendering plugin for Unreal Engine, Disguise, Pixotope
